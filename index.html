About

Colour is our craft.

Your Dream Shade was founded on a simple belief: that the right colour transforms a space. We spent years perfecting our six primary palettes, each spanning seven hand-crafted shades — from the palest tints to the deepest tones.

Every shade is mixed with premium pigments, tested for durability, and designed to work beautifully on its own or blended with others. Our online mixer lets you experiment before you commit — so you can find exactly what you're looking for.
